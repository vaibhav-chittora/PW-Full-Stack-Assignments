Operators in JavaScript are symbols or keywords used to perform operations on variables and values. They are crucial in programming for tasks such as calculations, comparisons, assignments, flow control, error handling, and data manipulation. Essentially, operators provide the means to perform various actions and operations within a JavaScript program, making them indispensable for writing effective and functional code.

Performing Operations: Operators allow you to perform various operations, such as arithmetic calculations, logical comparisons, etc.

Data Manipulation: Operators are fundamental for manipulating data, including concatenating strings, converting data types, etc
